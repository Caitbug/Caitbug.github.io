rap1993

rap1993 is another artist-driven storefront, for an artist and creator who specializes in custom designed ball-jointed dolls. This theme has been developed to work hand-in-hand with the Wordpress customizer tools, so that the artist can easily change site colors and imagery without further needing further development. A custom homepage acts as an entry into the site with information on the artist's upcoming events and portfolio, before leading into the shop itself where her limited-run products are featured.

Tokkisprout

The artist at tokkisprout wanted something bright and colorful, with hand drawn touches interspersed throughout. Little mushrooms that fill the basket as items are added to it are just a sampling of the quirky, personal touches interspersed throughout the theme created for this illustrator. Recently launched, there are still some finishing touches to be made, but the bold color blocked theme brings the feels of Tokkisprout's artist table from conventions to a modern, mobile-responsive storefront.
